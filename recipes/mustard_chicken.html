Mustard Chicken Recipe

Mustard Chicken recipe for air fryer.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs
  • 2 tbsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine Dijon Mustard, Honey, salt, and black pepper in small bowl. Stir to combine thoroughly.
  2. Place chicken thighs in resealable bag and coat with mixture from step 1
  3. Marinate chicken thighs in refrigerator for 15 minutes minimum, or up to 24 hours.
  4. Set air fryer to roast at 390Â°F for 20 minutes (27 minutes for double recipe, both sides air fryer). Flip chicken halfway through cooking.

These instructions may or may not be correct.
